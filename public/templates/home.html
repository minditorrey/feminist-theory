Welcome to Medical Ethics!

This site is where you and your peers from three sections of the course this fall will develop, illustrate, and organize your collective knowledge about issues in medical ethics.

The Role of the Professor

I will facilitate learning by providing the structure and organization of the course materials and activities. I am available as a resource to help students learn about the ethical issues involved in modern medical settings.

The Role of the Student

Students will engage with the course materials and collaborate with their peers through a variety of activities to develop knowledge on these interesting ethical issues.
